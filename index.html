Who We Are

AG7 Bathrooms Renovations Ltd is Manchester's premier destination for bespoke bathroom design and installation. Based in the heart of Greater Manchester, we’ve spent over a decade turning outdated spaces into modern luxury retreats.

Our team of dedicated specialists handles everything—from initial 3D design to the final tile placement. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and our ability to work within diverse budgets without compromising on the high-end look.
